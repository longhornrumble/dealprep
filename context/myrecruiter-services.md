# MyRecruiter AI - Service Capabilities Context

## What MyRecruiter Is

MyRecruiter is an AI-powered engagement system for nonprofits that provides 24/7 conversational AI chat widgets for websites. Our core value proposition: "Never miss another volunteer or donor inquiry again."

## Core Capabilities (What We CAN Do)

### 1. Conversational AI Chat Widget
- Embeddable chat widget on any website
- Natural language conversations with visitors
- Live in 48 hours with no technical skills needed
- Purpose-built for nonprofit use cases

### 2. Visitor Engagement & FAQ Handling
- 24/7 availability (nights, weekends, holidays)
- Answers common questions instantly
- Handles 49.4% of inquiries that come after business hours
- 65.6% conversion rate on inquiries

### 3. Form Collection Through Conversation
- Progressive field gathering via natural dialogue
- Volunteer signup forms
- Donor inquiry forms
- Contact/general inquiry forms
- Event registration forms

### 4. Lead Capture & CRM Integration
- Automatic capture of visitor information
- Email notifications to staff
- Integration with existing CRM systems
- Google Sheets export
- Webhook delivery to other systems

### 5. Knowledge Base Powered Responses
- RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) for accurate answers
- Responses based on organization's actual programs and information
- Consistent messaging aligned with organization's voice

## What We CANNOT Do (Out of Scope)

- Custom mentor-mentee matching algorithms
- Full volunteer management systems with scheduling
- Custom analytics dashboards beyond our standard offering
- Complex multi-system workflow automation
- Direct database integrations requiring custom development
- Mobile app development
- Custom CRM development
- Predictive analytics or machine learning model training

## Ideal AI Opportunity Framing

When suggesting AI opportunities, focus on:

1. **Website Chatbot for Visitor Engagement**
   - First point of contact for website visitors
   - Answer FAQs about programs, volunteering, donating
   - Available 24/7 when staff are not

2. **Conversational Form Collection**
   - Replace static forms with conversational capture
   - Higher completion rates through guided dialogue
   - Progressive disclosure of fields

3. **Automated Response to Inquiries**
   - Instant acknowledgment of volunteer interest
   - Immediate donor thank-you messages
   - Quick answers to common questions

4. **Lead Qualification & Routing**
   - Capture intent and route to appropriate staff
   - Triage inquiries by urgency/type
   - Reduce staff time on repetitive questions

## Proof Points

- 767% increase in volunteer signups (Austin Angels case study, first month)
- 8x more applications vs static forms
- 65.6% conversion rate
- 49.4% of inquiries handled outside business hours
